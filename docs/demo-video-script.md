# OrgVitals — 60-Second Demo Video Script

## Setup
- Screen recording of the web app
- Clean browser, no bookmarks bar
- Background music: upbeat, corporate-tech (low volume)
- Voiceover: confident, measured pace

---

## Script

### 0:00–0:05 — Problem Statement
**[Black screen, white text fades in]**
> Text: "Companies spend $500K+ on organizational health assessments. They take months. They're subjective. They're outdated on delivery."
> [Text fades out]

### 0:05–0:10 — Solution Intro
**[OrgVitals logo animates in, then transitions to the landing page]**
> Voiceover: "OrgVitals turns email data into instant organizational intelligence."

### 0:10–0:18 — Upload Flow
**[Cursor drags a file onto the upload area]**
> Voiceover: "Just upload your company's email data..."
> [Processing animation: "Parsing emails... Building communication graph... Computing analytics..."]
> Voiceover: "...and in seconds, you get a complete organizational diagnostic."
> [Animation completes, redirects to dashboard]

### 0:18–0:28 — Dashboard Reveal
**[Dashboard loads with animated health score gauge spinning to 62]**
> Voiceover: "Your org health score — 62 out of 100. Connectivity is strong, but there are critical bottleneck risks."
> [Camera pans down to metric cards and top critical people]
> Voiceover: "Instantly see who your most critical people are, which communities exist, and where waste is happening."

### 0:28–0:38 — Graph Explorer
**[Click "Graph" in sidebar, force graph loads and animates]**
> Voiceover: "Explore the actual communication network. Every node is a person, every edge is a relationship."
> [Zoom into a cluster, click a high-centrality node]
> Voiceover: "Color-coded by team, sized by importance. Click anyone to see their full profile."
> [Node panel slides in with metrics]

### 0:38–0:48 — AI Q&A
**[Click "Chat" in sidebar, chat interface appears]**
> Voiceover: "Ask any question about your organization in plain English."
> [Types: "What would happen if our most critical person left?"]
> [Streaming response appears with specific names, metrics, and impact analysis]
> Voiceover: "Powered by GraphRAG — combining graph analytics with GPT-5 for data-backed answers."

### 0:48–0:55 — Report Generation
**[Click "Reports" in sidebar, click "Generate Report"]**
> Voiceover: "Generate a complete diagnostic report in one click — executive summary, risk analysis, and actionable recommendations."
> [Report renders with formatted sections]

### 0:55–1:00 — Closing
**[Fade to black, OrgVitals logo]**
> Text: "OrgVitals — Your AI-Powered Chief of Staff"
> Text: "www.orgvitals.ai"
> [Logo holds for 2 seconds]
